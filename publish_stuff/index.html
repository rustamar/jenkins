DevOps Engineer

Category: 
Engineering
Location: 
NL - Amsterdam
Apply now
  • Share on Twitter
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on LinkedIn
  • Share via Email

The Role

This is a unique opportunity to step into a growing, transformational division of Pegasystems focused on delivering the latest and greatest DevOps tooling and techniques to both Pega and Pega’s customers.

The ideal DevOps engineer is a creative and adaptive individual who has a healthy mix of both development and operational skillsets. The candidate needs to thrive in a fast-paced, collaborative environment where successful associates are able to quickly develop game-changing ideas and apply innovative technologies to raise the bar in delivery excellence. 

Take a look at what our office is like in Amsterdam http://pe.ga/2eVvgMN

In this role, you will:

  • Work collaboratively with other engineering team members to implement, support and evangelise the latest DevOps [Office1] practices like Continuous Integration, Continuous Delivery/Deployment, visualisation, monitoring and automation. These solutions will enable Pega’s scaled agile delivery organisation (700+ people) to continually reduce time to market for customer features and rapidly react to market demands.
  • Leverage both 3rd party DevOps-centric technologies (Jenkins, Gradle, Artifactory, Git, Docker) as well as custom solutions to deliver and support a best-in-class development and delivery experience
  • Provide technical assistance to advance the capabilities of products and resolve complex issues

Pega Offers:

  • A rapidly growing yet well-established business
  • The world’s most innovative organisations as reference-able clients
  • Analyst acclaimed technology leadership in a massive emerging market
  • A highly collaborative environment for the designing and development of new ideas and feature

The Person:

The ideal candidate is self-motivated, inquisitive and creative, with a passion for continuous improvement and excellent people skills. This person can work independently as well as with a team, leads by example and demonstrates a commitment to quality development and operational practices.

Qualifications will include:

  • BA/BS degree in Computer Science or similar degree
  • Solid understanding of DevOps principles, tools and technology roadmap
  • Solid understanding of, and practical experience with, the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC)
  • Solid object-oriented design and development skills [Office2]
  • Have experience of working with public cloud providers and private cloud
  • Have grasp of virtualisation techniques and concepts
  • Have experience working with Docker ecosystem
  • Knowledge of and/or willingness to work within scrum, kanban and other agile methodologies
  • Ability to work collaboratively with both operational and development-minded teams

 We value diversity in our workforce and welcome enquiries from all suitably qualified applicants.
